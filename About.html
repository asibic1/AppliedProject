About Me

Hi, I'm a Senior at Northern Arizona University, currently studying for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Technology.

I've always had an interest in the field of computer technology and I find it fascinating.

Besides studying, I also enjoy amateur photography, as well as volunteering and giving back to the community.

In the past, I've helped clean and remodel a veteran’s home for “House of Heroes,” and was a guest speaker for “Children’s First Academy” on the importance of education. I also led a team of volunteers to organize a toy warehouse for W. Steven Martin 911 Toy Drive, and she has served meals to the homeless at St. Vincent De Paul.
